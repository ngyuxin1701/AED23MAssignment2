Strategies

  • Pets and food are grouped into Tiers based on their relative strength in the game. Tier 1 pets/food are the weakest overall and Tier 6 has the strongest abilities/effects in the game.

  • Your pets' abilities can be improved by levelling them up. This can be done by merging existing pets with duplicate pets from the shop.

    Level No. of Pets
    1 1
    2 +2 (3 in total)
    3 +3 (6 in total)

  • This section will list the Pets and Food according to their Tiers, then in alphabetical order. Note that you can purchase an expansion pack for more pets, but I will only be coversing strategies for the free standard pack.

Pets

GENERAL TIPS

  • In early game (i.e. Tiers 1-3), it is best for you to level up your pets quickly and sell them to buy new ones with better abilities. It can be useful to have multiple of the same pets of a lower tier in seperate slots and merge them when you go up a tier.

  • In late game (i.e. Tiers 4-6), it is best to stick to your pets and try to level it up or feed it food to boost its strength. But if you are a beginner, don't be afraid to try different pets to see which pet pairing work and which don't.

Food

GENERAL TIPS

  • Certain food pairs well with certain pets.

  • Feeding the pet at the end honey can increase likelihood of a win.

  • If possible, purchase food which has permanent abilities (e.g. apple) rather than food which has one time abilities (e.g. cupcake).

TIER 1

APPLE

Give a Pet +1/+1.

HONEY

Give a Pet a Honey Bee (1/1) after it faints.

TIER 2

CUPCAKE

Give a Pet +3/+3 until end of battle.

MEAT BONE

Give a Pet Bone Attack (deal extra 5 dmg).

SLEEPING PILL

Make a friendly Pet faint.

TIER 3

GARLIC
Give a Pet Garlic Armor (decrease incoming dmg by 2).

SALAD BOWL

Give 2 random Pets +1/+1.

TIER 4

CANNED FOOD

Give all current and future shop Pets +2/+1.

PEAR

Give a Pet +2/+2.

TIER 5

CHILLI
Give a Pet Splash Attack (deal extra 5 dmg to the pet behind).

CHOCOLATE

Give a Pet +1 Experience.

SUSHI

Give 3 random Pets +1/+1.

TIER 6

MELON
Give a Pet Melon Armor (decrease incoming dmg by 20 once).

MUSHROOM

Give a Pet Extra Life (revive as (1/1)).

PIZZA

Give 2 random Pets +2/+2.

STEAK

Give a Pet Steak Attack (Deal extra 20 dmg once).

OTHERS

COCONUT SHIELD

Negates all damage once (only obtainable by Gorilla).

MILK

Give a Pet +1/+2, +2/+4, +3/+6 (only obtainable by Cow).

POISON ATTACK

Knock out any animal hit by this (only obtainable by Scorpion).
