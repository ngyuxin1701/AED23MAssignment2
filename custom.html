Customizations

There are various customizations that you can make in the game. These might cost actual money or points. Each trophy you win earns you +5 points and if you win 10 rounds, each win is worth +6 points instead, netting you a grand total of 60 points.


PETS

The game starts you off on the free standard pack, but you can purchase the extension pack using actual money. However, the expansion pack is not needed to win the game as strategy play a greater role than the pets' abilities.




HATS & BACKGROUNDS

You can also customize the way your game looks by purchasing hats and/or backgrounds. They can cost acutal money or points. Note that they do not impact the stats of your pets and are for aesthetic purposes only.
