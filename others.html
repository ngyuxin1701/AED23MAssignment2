ACHIEVEMENTS

When you have levelled up your pet to level 3, you will get a sticker in your achievements gallery. Note that this does not give your pet any extra stats, but it is still nice to try various pets to explore their abilities and complete the gallery.




HISTORY

Under the history tab, you can access your Hall of Fame, Replay, and Stats.

  1. Hall of Fame
    Shows your best games in both Arena and Versus modes.

  2. Replay
    You can play back all your games here and analyse your team's performance.

  3. Stats
    Here you can see your game stats such as: matches played, matches won, trophies won, points won, and how many times you bought eat pet/food.
