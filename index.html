Super Auto Pets




Created by Team Wood Games, Super Auto Pets is a free-to-play multi-platform auto battler. Unlike most auto battlers existing today, Super Auto Pets prides its gameplay in understanding how each creature behaves rather than relying solely on their stats. With that said, Super Auto Pets can be quite complicated and intimidating in the very beginning. But never fear, dear pet battler; that's why I have decided to make this guide!
